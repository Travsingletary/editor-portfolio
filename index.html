I'm a senior video editor with 15+ years in television and streaming, cutting documentary, unscripted, and branded content for major networks including A&E, OWN, BET, and Discovery.

I shape raw footage into clear, compelling stories — building structure, tightening pacing, and landing emotion from assembly to final delivery. My edits move with intention: every cut earns its place.

Beyond the timeline, I've built my own distribution infrastructure, automated post-delivery pipelines, and integrated AI into production workflows — not to replace craft, but to protect the time that belongs to it.

Based in Los Angeles · Available immediately for remote engagements — long-form, series, specials, and branded campaigns.
